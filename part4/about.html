Origins

Big Sal's Subs, a beloved sandwich haven nestled in the heart of New York City, has been crafting mouthwatering sub sandwiches since 1903. Founded by an Italian family with a passion for creating culinary masterpieces, the aroma of freshly baked bread and the sizzle of savory fillings have graced the bustling streets of NYC for generations. Their time-honored recipes and dedication to quality have made Big Sal's a cherished institution where locals and tourists alike come to savor the essence of Italian flavors. With a legacy as rich and flavorful as their subs, Big Sal's Subs is a testament to the enduring magic of family traditions and the love for crafting the perfect sandwich.
